For Hibernate and JPA.
exampleHibernateJPA is made like example of use on JPA and Hibernate.
In instance configuration with JPA @Annotations.
In resources META-INF config files Hibernate.cfg.xml for Hibernate and Persistence.xml for JPA, for this we need Hibernate.jar or dependency in pom.xml.
All realization of JPA DAO inside jpaHibernateDAO.
JpaUtil is made for only one EntityManagerFactory on application.
